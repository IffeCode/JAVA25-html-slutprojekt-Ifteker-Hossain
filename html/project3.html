Puppies

A puppy is a young dog, typically under one year of age. They are born neurologically and immunologically underdeveloped and rely on their mother for care, learning to see, hear, and walk in their first few weeks. This period is crucial for development, including socialization and learning to interact with their environment and other creatures.
