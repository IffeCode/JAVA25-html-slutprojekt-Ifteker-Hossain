Kitten

Kittens are born blind and deaf, weigh about \(3-4\) ounces, and have blue eyes that change color over time. They rely entirely on their mother for survival, including warmth and stimulation to use the bathroom. Kittens develop quickly, begin to walk around three weeks old, and can start weaning from milk to solid food at about four to five weeks.
